About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my personal website.
I'm Madeleine, and I'm a Data Scientist living in New York City.
I am currently working as Senior Investment Data Scientist in Vanguard's Investment Management Group, on the Fintech Strategies team.

I studied Fundamental and Applied Mathematics in the Classes Préparatoire aux Grandes Ecoles (CPGE) at Lycée Janson-de-Sailly in Paris, France.
I then studied Computer Science at Telecom ParisTech, a French engineering school, where I had a double concentration in 1. Computer Science Theory and Operations Research and 2. Data Science in Machine Learning.
I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Telecom ParisTech and a Master's degree in Data Science from Columbia University, for which I moved to the US in 2019. I have been working full-time as a Data Scientist since 2020.

My areas of interest (work-related) include: (but are not limited to)
- Machine Learning, Deep Learning
- Natural Language Processing (old school and LLMs)
- Operations Research and Optimization (Linear Programming, Mixed Integer Programming)
- Graph Theory (and where it intersects with any of the above)
- Anomaly Detection for Time Series, Graphs and how it can be applied to Cybersecurity, Fraud Detection, Investment Management...
- Detection of AI-generated content (Deep Fakes, LLM-generated text...), which is for me at the interesection of {most technically interesting} x {most impact on ethical use of AI for the general public}.
